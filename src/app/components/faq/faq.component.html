FAQ

What is Volida?

Volida is a streaming service that gives users worldwide access to live events, music, and comedy. Watch award-winning artists' performances from the comfort of your home. It's quite easy to get started! Create an account, buy the live stream, and watch!

How does Volida work?

Volida can be viewed on any device with an internet connection. Volida is compatible with any smartphone, tablet, computer, or laptop.

Can I purchase a show from Nigeria?

Volida streams can be purchased in Nigeria. Simply create a flutterwave barter dollar card, which will allow you to make payments anywhere in the world where your local card would not due to restrictions. Please see here for further information on how to make a barter card.
